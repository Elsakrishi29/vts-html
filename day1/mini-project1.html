Namlabs Announces Continued Growth of Atatus and ReplyBird to Empower Modern Businesses

March 2, 2026

Namlabs, a fast-growing technology company, today announced the continued expansion of its product portfolio, including its flagship platforms Atatus and ReplyBird. The company remains committed to helping businesses improve application performance, customer engagement, and overall digital experience.

Atatus, Namlabs’ Application Performance Monitoring (APM) platform, enables developers and DevOps teams to monitor application performance, track real-time errors, and gain deep visibility into cloud infrastructure. With its intuitive dashboard and powerful diagnostics, Atatus helps teams quickly identify issues and maintain high-performing applications.

ReplyBird, the company’s customer communication and feedback platform, helps businesses collect customer reviews, manage online reputation, and respond efficiently to customer feedback. By streamlining communication workflows, ReplyBird empowers organizations to strengthen customer relationships and improve brand trust.

Namlabs continues to invest in innovation, focusing on scalable cloud solutions, enhanced analytics capabilities, and seamless integrations. The company aims to support startups, mid-sized businesses, and enterprises in navigating the evolving digital landscape.

About NamLabs

Namlabs is a technology-driven company focused on building reliable and scalable SaaS products. Through its platforms Atatus and ReplyBird, the company serves customers globally by delivering monitoring, analytics, and customer engagement solutions.
